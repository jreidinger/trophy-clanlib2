4.5  Level Definition

The last layer of your map is called 'lift'. On this layer you can define the levels of your track. Trophy knows two different levels: the top level for cars which are on bridges and the bottom level for all other cars. Note that a car stays on the same level if it leaves a zone where the level was defined and enters a zone where no level is defined. Further it's important, that a player is on the bottom layer before it drives under a bridge respectively a player has to be on the top layer before it drive on a bridge.

Top level zones are drawn in color #000100 for bottom level and #000200 for top levels.

Fig. 9: Example for the layer 'lift'.

You should avoid coloring regions which contain road parts on the top and such on the bottom level. Be generously with the distance to the trickily parts (as shown in the figure).
